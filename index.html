About Us

OUR PROGRAM

WHO WE ARE

Connect Theatre is a youth theatre company in Dundas, Ontario. We are a not-for-profit educational organization that has been offering youth an opportunity to come together and learn and grow through the performing arts! Our board and creative team work together to create community by engaging youth in theatre, and helping them grow as communicators, collaborators and actors!

WHAT WE DO

Our mission is to spark creativity, build confidence, and bring people together through the magic of musical theatre. We create a fun, welcoming space where young people can explore their talents, push their boundaries, and experience the joy of working as a team. We teach youth to enjoy the journey, and the end result just happens to be an incredible show.

OUR STYLE

For those who may not have found their place in traditional team settings, we offer a creative outlet where they can shine, connect, and grow. Our productions are more than just performances‚Äîthey're opportunities for youth to discover their potential, develop leadership and people skills, and create something they'll be proud of long after the final curtain call.

Most importantly, we believe in the power of the arts to foster community.

"We empower youth by creating a place where everyone belongs, everyone contributes, and everyone works together on the shared goal of bringing an amazing performance to the stage!"
