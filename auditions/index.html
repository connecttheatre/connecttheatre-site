Financial Assistance Available

The participation fee is $250, which includes your membership to Connect Theatre and show merchandise! If this fee is a barrier for you to participate, please note: we have a bursary program and are able to offer financial assistance and payment plans to those who may require it. We firmly believe that finances should never be a barrier to youth who want to perform in community theatre!

Connect Theatre is an equal opportunity not-for-profit youth organization. Performers aged 8–28 of all identities and expressions are invited to show us their skills.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need to play an instrument?

While School of Rock features musicians, you don't need to play an instrument to audition! We can teach you or pair you with the right role. If you do play guitar, bass, drums, or keyboards, definitely let us know!

What if I can't make all the rehearsal dates?

We understand conflicts happen. Please list any known conflicts on your audition form. We work with families to accommodate occasional absences, but consistent attendance is important for ensemble numbers.

Is there a callback audition?

Yes! Based on video submissions, we'll invite select performers to callbacks where we'll work on scenes and ensemble numbers together. Callbacks are low-pressure and actually quite fun!

Can parents watch auditions?

Absolutely! We pride ourselves on creating a welcoming atmosphere. Parents are welcome to accompany their performer into the audition room if desired.
